Sajhe Sapne

Sajhe Sapne is a non profit organisation who supports rural women and girls who wanted to live their dreams. It gives job training so that they can fulfill their dreams. This is 9 months residensial program.

The 3 courses are:-

Umang: Management Development

Launch your career in the field of social work & general project management in different role Our alumni work as project associates, facilitators, operations associates in nonprofits, social enterprises, and companies.

Tarang: Web Development

Launch your career in the field of front end web development We hope that our alumni will work in companies and social enterprises as front end web developers.

Aarohan: Math Educator

Launch your career in the field of primary math teaching & educational content creation. Our alumni work as math educators and facilitators in schools, non-profits, and private tuitions for kids.
